The Golden Rules of Netiquette

The Golden Rule

Treat others as you would like to be treated.

No Flaming

Do not verbally abuse, intentionally attack, or disrespect another person online

Don't Plagiarize

When you borrow something from someone, give them credit. Site their name or their site.

No Spamming

Do not send unwanted electronic messages or sending the same message over and over.

Keep Personal Info
Personal

Do not reveal sensitive information online i.e. name, age, address, social security number.

Don't Overuse Emojis

Avoid overuse of emoticons. They lose their cuteness when overused and tend to irritate people.

Keep it PG

Don't Overuse Caps!

Respect Others

Be Careful of
what you Post
